Stepwise set up guide to get started with Trezor Wallet 

At the beginning of your crypto trading journey with Trezor Wallet, you need to make sure that you know all the comprehensive details about setting up your wallet from scratch. So, what are you supposed to do in this scenario: 

  1. In the beginning, you must go to trezor.io/start
  2. Now, you must choose your Trezor Wallet model from the given options 
  3. Then, you should download the Trezor Bridge on your device 
  4. After the Bridge is installed, use the USB cable to connect your device to the PC 
  5. Then, choose the option to set up your wallet 
  6. Store the 12 or 24 words Secret Recovery Phrase carefully 
  7. Set up your PIN and you're good to go
